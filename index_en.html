Look through care advices

Don`t use any heat devices to dry felted boots! Also, we don`t recommend to wear wet boots. Make sure your boots are dry before walk.

Use cardboard or paper package to store valenki. Before it make sure they absolutely dry. You can cover felted boots with special agent against moths for storage. Also we recommend to use devices to fix boots form.

Clean felted boots with the brush. At the same time you can wash them in washing machine at delicate program.

Why our felted boots?

What can be better than stylish, unique, comfortable and made of natural fabric footwear? Want to know more? Just try our boots!

Our boots are made of 100% felted wool. The unique feature of felted wool is temperature and humidity regulation. You will feel comfortable either if it`s frost outside or early spring day. The temperature range is from +15C to -30C!

Felts is slightly rough but it`s rather advantage – these boots have massage effect. Our boots don’t stab but if it`s uncomfortable to wear first time don`t mention it – felted wool is coming more soft the more you wear it.

Another great thing about felts is it`s lightweight and soft despite the thickness. Boots insole has additional structure under your heel which is uplift it to make our boots more comfortable to wear like you walk in slippers.

The boots sole are made of EVA 0,59 inches. EVA is ecological foamed rubber, very light, elastic and endurable. As it’s wavy the boots don`t slide.

The boots don`t need special care. Don`t worry if they are drabble! Just let them dry and clean the dirt with the brush. If it’s cold outside the slush don`t stick to the boots but roll down. At the same time you can wash them in washing machine at delicate program.
